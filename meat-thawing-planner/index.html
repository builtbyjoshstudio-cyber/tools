Cold Water Thawing

Faster thawing. Requires active water management and immediate cooking.

Estimated Time
--
  • Submerge in a sealed leak-proof bag.
  • Change cold tap water every 30 minutes.
  • Must cook immediately after thawing is complete.
⚠️ Critical Seafood Safety: If your seafood is vacuum-sealed, you MUST puncture or remove the packaging completely before thawing in the refrigerator or cold water to eliminate the risk of lethal anaerobic botulism bacteria growth.
